# Spaghettini/Tagliolini alla "Tognazzi"

## Ingredients

- 1/2 lemon juice
- 1/2 grated lemon rind
- 2 tbsps butter
- 3 tbsps cream
- 3 tbsps parmesan
- salt & pepper

## Instructions

While the pasta is cooking, prepare the sauce, gently melting the butter and cream at low temperarure in a pan, add the lemon juice, salt, pepper and the lemon rind.
Strain the pasta and dress it with the sauce and parmesan.
