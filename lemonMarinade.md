# Lemon marinade for Lamb

These are two attempts to replicate an asian lamb marinade.

## Ingredients (version 1)

- 2 small lemons
- ~ 200 ml honey
- 2 tbsp raw cane sugar
- 2 cap wine vinegar
- 3 dash salt
- dash tabasco
- dash worcestershire
- dash soya

## Instructions

Mix ingredients to taste and use to marinate lamb before barbecuing.

## Ingredients (version 2)

This is the very simple version I usually use.
Adjust the ingredients to taste.

- 1/2 lemon
- 2 tbspn honey
- 2 tbspn soya sauce

## Instructions

Heat the ingredients in a small pan over a very low heat to melt the honey and amalgamate it with the lemon juice and soya sauce.
Use to marinate lamb ribs before barbecuing.
