# Meta's Dutch Potato Salad

## Ingredients

- 5 (large) potatoes
- 1 bunch green onions
- 2 (large) onions
- 3 fresh tomatoes
- 1 green pepper
- 1.5 lb
- (~.7 kg) lean ground beef
- 4 5 eggs
- 5 (large) pickles
- 15-20 pickled onions
- 1 (large) canned/jarred beets
- 4 large tbl mayonnaise
- 1 tbl vinegar
- pepper, salt, thyme, oregano, paprika, parsley, chives ...

## Instructions

- Boil and mash the potatoes
- Scald, peel and chop the tomatoes
- Boil the eggs and chop them with an egg chopper
- Chop the pickles, onions and green pepper
- Fry the hamburger and cooking onions with the spices
- Mash the whole mess up together
- Let cool

## Note

Do the chopping by hand, not with a cuisinart, or you'll get an unpleasant gluey paste
