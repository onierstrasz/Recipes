# Soha's Naans

## Ingredients

- 1 kg flour
- 1 tbsp dry yeast
- 1 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- 200 cc milk 
- 200 cc water
- 2 tbsp butter or margarine.

## Instructions

Makes 11-12 nan.

- Combine ingredients, cover and let dough rise 2 hours.
- Talk small chunks and make flat naans on a floured board.
- Put batches in oven at high temperature under grill for ~ 1 minute (both sides).

## Variant

Add a bit of yoghurt to the dough.
